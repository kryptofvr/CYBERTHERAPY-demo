What is CYBERTHERAPY?

Welcome to CYBERTHERAPY, a comprehensive platform dedicated to helping those who have been affected by cyberbullying and online harassment. Our goal is to provide a safe and supportive environment for cyberbullying victims and survivors to express their emotions and traumatic experiences without fear of judgment or ridicule. Here, individuals will find a valuable resource center filled with guides on cyberbullying prevention, as well as mental, emotional, and physical health tips. Additionally, we offer educational materials such as homework help and college advice, aiming to empower and uplift our community. Through peer-to-peer interactions, forums, and social engagement, users can connect with others who share similar experiences, offering advice and emotional support. Safety is our utmost priority, and our website is equipped with crisis prevention and intervention protocols, alongside relevant contact information for hotlines and helplines, ensuring immediate help and support in times of emergency. At CYBERTHERAPY, we stand together in solidarity, fostering healing and resilience for all those affected by cyberbullying.
